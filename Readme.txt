Controls:
Space to fire Spinning Top
Press C to change Type Camera View
Camera 1 - Follow Camera
Camera 2 - Player View
Camera 3 - Overhead View
Camera 4 - Top View
 
Left and Right Key for moving Left and Right
Up and Down Key for Changing Launching Angle

If Lattu(Spinning Top) hits the Target then score get incremented by 10 points
and target reappears at another position otherwise 10 score is deducted.

In terminal firstly-
1) Enter "make" command
2) Enter "./game"
